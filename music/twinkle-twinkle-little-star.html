Twinkle Twinkle Little Star

By Susam Pal on 14 Feb 2013

This is a short musical piece consisting of 56 measures. It is 1 minute 52 seconds long. The music is composed of four tracks.

MP3 Score Lilypond Details

The four tracks in this piece are: grand piano, slow strings, xenon pad and music box.

This arrangement is based on the popular melody of the nursery rhyme called Twinkle, twinkle, little star. The melody is played with the treble notes of the piano. I wrote the bass notes for the piano and strings. The high notes for the pad and music box bring in the emotions of love and happiness. I recorded this after about two hours of practice.
