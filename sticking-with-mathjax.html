Sticking With MathJax

By Susam Pal on 13 Feb 2020

I have considered switching from MathJax to KaTeX for my MathJax-based projects such as TeXMe and MathB but I had to decide against doing so because MathJax supports commands like \label, \eqref, \notag, etc. and environments like align, multiline, etc. KaTeX does not support them, so switching to KaTeX would break many existing documents written using TeXMe or MathBin. I am going to stick with MathJax for now.
