Susam's Computation Club

Welcome to this computation club! This is a tiny and informal hobby group that picks classics from the field of mathematics and computation, reads them, and discusses them together.

Join

To join this our discussions, visit the link below to join our Matrix channel. All meeting schedule updates are announced in this channel:

#susam:matrix.org

In case you are familiar with IRC networks and like it more than Matrix, then you might want to join #susam on the Libera IRC network instead. The Matrix and IRC channels are bridged together with a relay bot, so messages posted to one channel are automatically forwarded to the other channel. If you are not an active IRC user, consider joining the Matrix channel instead because it is more convenient for someone unfamiliar with IRC.
